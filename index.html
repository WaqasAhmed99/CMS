Committee Rules

Rule 1

Har month ki 10 Tarikh se pehle member committee jama karwade.

Rule 2

Har member ko committee ki raqam month ki 15 Tarikh tak mil jayegi.

Rule 3

Agar koi member committee se nikalta hai, toh uski bhari hui raqam committee khatam hone ke baad milegi.

Rule 4

Agar kisi member ne committee bar waqt ada nahi ki, toh usey committee se nikala ja sakta hai.
